Shipping Policy

At Trendifyo, we are committed to delivering your products efficiently and reliably. Please review our shipping policy below for details on delivery times, costs, and procedures.

1. Shipping Zones and Carriers

We currently ship to all major cities and towns across India. For international shipping inquiries, please contact our support team. We partner with reputable logistics providers such as Delhivery, Blue Dart, and XpressBees to ensure your orders arrive safely.

2. Processing Time

  • All orders are processed within 1-2 business days (excluding weekends and holidays) after receiving your order confirmation email.
  • You will receive another notification when your order has shipped.
  • During peak seasons or promotional periods, processing times may be slightly longer.

3. Shipping Rates and Delivery Estimates

Shipping charges for your order will be calculated and displayed at checkout. We offer the following shipping options:

  • Standard Shipping:
    • Delivery Time: 5-7 business days
    • Cost: ₹99 (Free for orders above ₹999)
  • Express Shipping:
    • Delivery Time: 2-3 business days
    • Cost: ₹199

Please note that delivery estimates are calculated from the date of shipment, not the date of order placement.

4. Order Tracking

Once your order has shipped, you will receive an email notification from us which will include a tracking number you can use to check its status. Please allow 24-48 hours for the tracking information to become available.

You can track your order directly on our Track Order page.

5. Shipping Address Changes

If you need to change your shipping address after placing an order, please contact us immediately at support@trendifyo.com. We will do our best to accommodate your request, but we cannot guarantee changes once the order has been processed or shipped.

6. Damaged or Lost Packages

Trendifyo is not liable for any products damaged or lost during shipping. If you received your order damaged, please contact the shipment carrier to file a claim. Please save all packaging materials and damaged goods before filing a claim.

If your package is lost in transit, please contact us after the estimated delivery window has passed, and we will assist you in contacting the carrier.
